ALOVAS - A.I. Pathology Platform

Toward Better & More Intelligent Medical Practice

We provides a new way of digital pathology platform to enhance pathologists workflow.

Cross-platform Giga-pixel Viewer

Web-based WSI pathology image viewer with smart prefetch provides responsive and seamless zooming up to 40X. User-friendly WSI organizer with tiles for quick search and list for details. Provides intuitively WSI annotations labelling and multi WSI view for direct comparison. Collaboration friendly with mobile device support and even work from home.

A.I. based Analysis Platform

Industry leading deep learning modules combined with image analysis using Tensorflow. Multi format digitize slide compatible. Scalable and robust cloud architecture that provides sustain platform stability with partnership.

Scanner and Storage Support

Provides scanning services with open-slide multiple DICOM based formats to digitize WSI and scanned file delivery. Private pathology WSI cloud storage with security embedded and A.I. algorithms services for digital WSI slide diagnosis review.

Our Product Features

ALOVAS acts as an A.I. Pathology Platform which simultaneously refers to cell level features and tissue structure to provide detection of liver disease such as hepatitis, fatty liver and tumor. The integrated algorithms aid doctors in quickly identifying problems of the patients, reducing the effort of diagnosis and the bias from subjected judgement. Our platform provides high resolution live image viewer with automated workflow. Annotations and structural reports can also be made directly on the platform, accelerating diagnosis on liver pathology images.

Automatically highlight suspected tumor area and grading for accelerated diagnosis. Display various properties such as area, cell size, nuclear-cytoplasmic ratio of the detected tumor.

Deliver detected hepatic portals as bounding boxes and real-time density estimation of lymphocytes within selected portal helps with infection diagnosis.
