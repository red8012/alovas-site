Our Partnership

It is in your hands to create a better world for all who live in it.
With a hand from others, we grow stronger.

Ministry of Science and Technology

The Ministry of Science and Technology(MOST), originally established as the National Science Council(NSC), is the government agency dedicated to scientific and technological development. Its three main missions are promoting nationwide S&T development, supporting academic reasearch, and developing science parks. MOST is the main support to our fund.

National Cheng Kung University

National Cheng Kung University(NCKU) is positioned as a university of global importance that develops high quality, well-rounded citizens. "Pursuit of truth through exhaustive reasoning" is NCKU's motto, and the school therefore features a very down-to-earth academic climate conducive to steadfast pursuit of knowledge and truth. Our team is build from here.

National Cheng Kung University Hospital

National Cheng Kung University Hospital is a national hospital that offering excellent medical service to public, offering clinical teaching and internship, promoting medical research and development, assisting the development of local medical units and institutions, and holding continuous medical education programs for medical personnel. Our team is proudly collaborate with NCKU Hospital for collecting cases and medical technical support.

Kaohsiung Veterans General Hospital

Kaohsiung Veterans General Hospital aim to deliver high-quality,warm, and reliable healthcare, and cultivate well-skilled,, ethical medical teams. Their missions included develop science and technology to benefit both research and clinical medicine, and promote cooperation between medical services in the community and raise healthcare standards. Our team has supported with collecting cases and medical technical support by them.

Taiwan Semiconductor Research Institute

Taiwan Semiconductor Research Institute(TSRI) is in reponse to the introudciont of the 3-nm node and rapid development of new applications (e.g. artificial intelligence, quantum computers, next-gen magnetic RAM, high-speed computers, and 5G new network). TSRI is our back bone.

Taiwan Computing Cloud

Taiwan Computing Cloud delivers Taiwania2 as an AI supercomputer which built by a team of large Taiwanese hardware firms in collaboration with the NCHC of NARLabs of MOST. Its superior 9PFLOPS placed it 20th on the TOP500 list and 10th on the Green500 list of high-performance computers in the world, both the best marks that any Taiwanese supercomputer has ever achieved. Our platform is supported by TWCC.
